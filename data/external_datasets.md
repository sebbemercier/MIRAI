# Recommended Datasets for MIRAI (Sales & Interaction)

- **Bitext Retail/E-commerce Dataset**: Training dataset for customer support and sales bots. [Hugging Face](https://huggingface.co/datasets/bitext/Bitext-retail-ecommerce-llm-chatbot-training-dataset)
- **Agent-Customer Chat Dataset**: 12k text-based conversations for retail. [FutureBeeAI](https://www.futurebeeai.com/dataset/agent-customer-chat-dataset-for-retail-ecommerce)
- **SalesBot 2.0**: Focused on user willingness and sales dialogue. [GitHub](https://github.com/ictnlp/SalesBot)
